The Model

Public knowledge ETL

The system separates raw intake from reusable thought. Sources are inputs, captures preserve rough extraction, atoms hold defensible ideas, and artifacts are the public outputs.

1. Source

Something worth learning from

Books, papers, talks, conversations, and other inputs enter here with status, domain, progress, and provenance.

2. Capture

The first extraction surface

Highlights, fragments, reactions, excerpts, and rough synthesis get preserved before they are compressed into cleaner ideas.

3. Atom

Reusable unit of thought

Atoms hold claims, mechanisms, boundaries, and provenance so the graph compounds instead of turning into a stack of summaries.

4. Artifact

Public-facing output

Slides, notes, and other public artifacts turn internal learning into something legible and useful for anyone else.
